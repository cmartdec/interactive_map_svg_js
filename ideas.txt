Wildfire

Heatwaves
Meteorite Impact
Nuclear Accident
Iceberg (ships do collide against them)

GAMEPLAY:
---
player has to kill as many people as possible by creating natural disasters
player stars by creating a first natural disaster on a specific country
player gets danger points (yellow points on the country affected?) by killing people
player can enhance/improve natural disasters stats to be more deadly with danger points
player gets disasters points to unlock new natural disasters
player has to play strategically to not run out of disasters points (for that, he must be killing people non stop)
